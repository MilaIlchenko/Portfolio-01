    • About

      The website “Reparer din vaskemaskine” is a group project about a service guide for washing machine repairs. Each team member has assigned responsibilities. In my role, I focused on researching needs and interviewing people. In addition to this, I played a role in the design process, creating sketches , mockups and wrote code for website pages.

    • Task

      The website should be user-friendly and provide a cost-free service guide. Users should have the ability to get help anytime, anywhere with ease.

    • Problem solving

      Free website, guides through chatbot and videos. Users can save time and extend the life of the machine without buying a new one. Which is a positive aspect of reducing the production of washing machines and, accordingly, the negative impact on the environment.

    • Tools

      Trello, Figma, Photoshop, VSCode, and Git/GitHub, HTML,/CSS
